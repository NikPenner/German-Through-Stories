Use the following actions to give commands to Wolfgang’s Roboter

What would you like him to do?

Although separable prefixes are in front of the verb in the infinitive (e.g., aufstehen - to get up), the imperative with them works just like in English:

Model: “Roboter, steh auf und geh vorwärts!”

get up! → steh auf!

turn on! → schalte ein!

Actions: trinken, gehen, denken an (to think about), kaufen, schließen, zuhören, aufhören, einreichen, machen (to do)

Hint: Don't forget to mark the object for beign the object (use the second line from the rese-nese chart), e.g. Trink den Tee!
