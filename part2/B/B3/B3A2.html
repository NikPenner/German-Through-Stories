Aufgabe 2. Fill in the blanks

Watch out! This exercise targets the verbs that change a bit when used with du or er/sie/es (remember the 3 pattens:lesen> er liest, fallen>er fällt, geben>gibt?). To make it even trickier, I inserted a couple of 'normal' verbs here and there.
