Aufgabe 5. Siegfried and Wolfgang are finally participating in Speeddating in 1390. However, they came late because they had to do all those silly things for Maroona, so they only have one chance at meeting someone. Help them formulate the questions for their date and find out as much as possible about the other person.

Use ‘gern’ and the action & the question word in brackets to form questions and, of course, use the du-form.

Note: Beispiel. Gern trinken, was: Was trinkst du gern?

2. Gern hören, welche Musik:
3. Gern reisen, wohin:
4. Gern spielen, was:
Bonus questions. Watch out: these actions change slightly with du and er/sie/es:
5. Gern essen, was:
6. Gern sehen, welche Filme:
7. Gern lesen, welche Bücher:
